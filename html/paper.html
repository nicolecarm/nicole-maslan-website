The Creative Process

While working at the Claremont College's Sontag Center for Collaboration and Creativity, also known as The Hive, I dove into design thinking and strengthened my creative muscles. I co-led a workshop on "Designing your Life" and took a semester long course in Human-Centered Design.

Since working at The Hive, I have stretched my creativity in new ways, learning how to build websites, record and edit muisic with DAWs, and create vector images using graphic design software.
